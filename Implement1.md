# 🧩 Part 3: Role-Specific Implementation Analysis

## Architect – Protocol Implementation
**Challenge:** TCP/IP cannot transmit continuous neural states and lacks mathematical biological bindings.  
**Goals:**
- Develop TTP Layer & Contextual Framing.
- Establish Math Formalization for the protocol.
- Set strict Protocol Maturity & Quality Metrics (QoS).

## Security Specialist – Brain Firewall
**Challenge:** Identity compromise risk and complex cognitive injection attacks.  
**Goals:**
- Prevent thought manipulation.
- Integrate DAFT [Extended Edition] for extreme vulnerability testing.
- Design the System Halt / HITL Override mechanism.

## BCI Engineer – Hardware & Interface
**Challenge:** Signal interpretation accuracy and chaotic biological variables.  
**Goals:**
- Evaluate Non-Invasive BCI & map signals to Neural Dictionary.
- Lead the Domain Interface Mapping (Bio/Phy/Neuro to Math).
- Ensure hardware meets the minimum Maturity Metrics.

## Neuroethics Lead – System Boundaries
**Challenge:** Individual vs Hive Mind boundary in automated systems.  
**Goals:**
- Separate private thought space from shared cognition.
- Translate Ethics and Regulations into System Codes.
- Define boundaries for Human-In-The-Loop (HITL) interventions.

## Quantum Specialist – Decoding Pipeline
**Challenge:** Quantum timeline uncertainty and multidimensional data structures.  
**Goals:**
- Temporary AI/Supercomputer fallback.
- Long-term QKD security architecture.
- Integrate Quantum States into the overarching Math Formalization.
